About Us

Our Story: Founded in 2025 by Truphenah Abere, a daughter of small-scale farmers, who witnessed firsthand how smallholder farmers in Kenya lose up to 40% of their profits to intermediaries.

The Problem: Approximately 40% to 80% of Kenya's population depend on agriculture, yet farmers struggle with poor market access, price volatility, and exploitation by middlemen who control distribution channels.

Our Solution: AgriLink is a digital platform that connects farmers directly to retailers, restaurants, and consumers, providing real-time market prices, logistics support, and mobile payment solutions.

Impact: We've helped over 15,000 farmers increase their income by an average of 35% while providing fresh, traceable produce to urban markets across Kenya and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions

Farmers can register for free using their mobile phone by texting "JOIN" to our shortcode. Once registered, they can list their produce, receive real-time market prices, get connected to buyers, and receive payments directly to their mobile money accounts. Our logistics network handles pickup and delivery, so farmers don't need to worry about transportation.

We currently operate in Kenyacovering over 10 counties and districts. Our service areas include major agricultural regions like Central and Western Kenya. We're rapidly expanding and plan to cover all of East Africa by 2027.

Registration is completely free for both farmers and buyers. We charge a small 3% transaction fee only when a sale is completed. This is much lower than traditional middlemen who typically take 30-40% of the farmer's profits. There are no hidden fees, monthly charges, or upfront costs.

We have a dedicated support team available 24/7 through our hotline. If there are quality issues, delivery problems, or payment disputes, we investigate immediately and provide solutions. We also have an insurance program that protects both farmers and buyers from losses due to unforeseen circumstances.
